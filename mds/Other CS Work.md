In the Summer of 2025 I worked at two places. 

## Operations Research 
 I was working at IIT Delhi's Public Systems Lab on a UNFP funded project on optimizing food grain distribution in the Indian state of Odisha. This project involved learning the basics of operations research, processing a large amount of data, and presenting it to the user. 

For my demonstration prototype I used [CoinOR](https://www.coin-or.org/) for solving the system. At the time of me working there the actual project was still deciding between a few map API options, I used a local database taken from [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/) with [OSRM](https://project-osrm.org/) for my branch. We used this to compare if the enterprise options were giving similar results to the open source data. 

I built [Flask](https://flask.palletsprojects.com/en/stable/) app to display the optimal paths found.
